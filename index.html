About

Liz Townsend is a Full Stack Web Developer with a background in Graphic Design. She holds a certificate in Full Stack Web Development from KU and a BA in Studio Art from UMKC. She has created several full stack apps utilizing dynamically created content accessing both SQL and noSQL databases and third party APIs. A dedicated employee, she has been in her current position for over 15 years, and is highly organized and maintains brand consistency across all platforms. A lifelong learner, she is always eager to expand her skill set and master new techniques and looks forward to merging her design aesthetic with dynamic content to create elegant, eye-catching, fully integrated web sites.
