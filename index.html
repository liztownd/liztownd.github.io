Liz Townsend is a Full Stack Web Developer with a background in Graphic Design. She holds a BA in Studio Art from UMKC and a certificate in Full Stack Web Development from KU Coding Boot Camp. During the Boot Camp she created several APIs utilizing JS dynamically created content. Using skills gained in the Boot Camp she designed a responsive website for a yearly tradeshow. A dedicated employee, she has been in her current position for over 15 years, and is highly organized and maintains brand consistency across all platforms. A lifelong learner, she is always eager to expand her skill set and master new techniques and looks forward to merging her design aesthetic with dynamic content to create elegant, eye-catching, fully integrated web sites.

She has a daughter and 2 cats and volunteers with Girl Scouts and the PTA. In her spare time she likes to knit and read.
